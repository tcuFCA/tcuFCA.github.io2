TCU FCA Night of Champions Banquet






Event details

Every year, TCU FCA hosts a banquet to celebrate all God has done throughout the year and to thank our faithful donors. Join us for a family oriented celebration in the Sam Baugh Indoor Practice Facility, complete with dinner, a speaker, and numerous student-athlete testimonies.

  • Thursday | 14 April, 2016
  • 6:30 Doors Open, Dinner at 7:00PM
  • TCU Sam Baugh Indoor Practice Facility
  • For more information, contact Chauncey Franks: TCU FCA Life and Character Coach
    (214)-621-6448
    cfranks@fca.org
    2001 W. Plano Pkwy, Ste 3650 Plano, TX 75075
