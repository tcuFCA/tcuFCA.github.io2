Where We Meet

TCU FCA weekly gatherings are held every Monday night at 8:00pm in the Moncrief Club (Second Level) on the south side of Amon G. Carter Stadium.

For questions about directions, contact Chauncey Franks at 214-621-6448 or  cfranks@fca.org
